1. how to make a sponge cake.

  2. ingridients

  3. 175g of sugar,butter and flour
  4. 3 medium eggs
  5. baking powder

    how to make

  1. first mix togather the butter and sugar.
  2. next add the eggs and beat until smooth and creamy
  3. now mix the the the baking powder in with the flour
  4. then sift the flour into the butter mix and gently fold in.
  5. finally spoon the mixture into shallow cake tins and bake in medium oven for 25 minutes
