Musical chair - Games for all

How to play musical chair. For playing the game gather all your friends, say total 10 children
  1. Put 9 chairs in a line
  2. let 10 people go around them and play music
  3. when the music stops every one should sit on the chairs
  4. Whosoever did not get the chair will get eliminated
  5. Reduce one chair now and play game following steps 1-5
  6. Last one to sit in the chair wins
