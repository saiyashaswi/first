Musical chair - Games for all

How to play musical chair. For playing the game gather all your friends, say total 10 children
  1. Put 9 chairs in a line
  2. let 10 people go around them and play music
  3. when the music stops every one should sit on the chairs
  4. Whosoever did not get the chair will get eliminated
  5. Reduce one chair now and play game following steps 1-5
  6. Last one to sit in the chair wins

Inventors: Thomas Alva Edison

World’s greatest inventors
Thomas Alva Edison

Edison was an inventor and born in America. When he was the age of twelve he worked as a news seller. He printed all news paper in a train car.
Once one of his experiments went wrong and set fire to train car, the conductor sent Edison out of the train with his things.
When he was 21 years old he invented the first telegraph, later he invented the telegraph which could record sound, he was called the wizard of menlo park.
Edison failed for 3000 times for a light bulb after that he gave us the light bulb
